An aspiring Broadcaster

I’m Mc Vinzynth Lopez, a first-year broadcasting student with a love for storytelling and a fascination with how media shapes our world. But my journey didn’t start in front of a microphone or behind a camera. In fact, it began long before I ever knew broadcasting would become my career. About Me

"The journey before the mic turns to be my companion." I’m Mc Vinzynth Lopez, and my story starts in a small town where creativity thrived in the most unexpected places - Igbaras. Before broadcasting became my focus, I was a kid who didn’t know what ambition to hold and what dream to make, clueless how to create my own world. But then I found myself fascinated by documentaries and films. The idea that a single story could shape someone's perspective or change the way we perceive the world around us blew my mind. But it wasn’t until I started to join radio broadcasting contest that I realized broadcasting was where I truly belonged. I had found my voice, and I knew it was time to share it with the world. Now, as I take my first steps into broadcasting school, I’m ready to bring my unique perspective and passion to the field.
